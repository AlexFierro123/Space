What is a Blackhole?

"Black holes are fascinating cosmic phenomena. Think of them as ultra-powerful vacuum cleaners in space. They're formed when massive stars collapse under their own gravity. Anything that comes near, even light, gets pulled in so strongly that it can't escape. It's truly a mysterious and awe-inspiring aspect of our universe."
